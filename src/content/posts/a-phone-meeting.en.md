Held a conference call today with JJ's classmate.. lessons learned as follows (in chronological order

1. Leave enough time for the attendees to arrive.. in other words, schedule it as late as you can.. and don't stand anybody up

2. In a place like Kingsoft.. meeting rooms must be booked! Especially the phone and video conference rooms

3. Code monkeys, beyond matters of technology.. are not to be relied upon

4. From the above it follows that you'd better understand some tech yourself

5. When there's a big shot's leg to cling to, cling to it.. and if you feel yourself flagging, just ask the boss to ride out
