As if it had all been arranged last century, so this life could be spent sharing its delights\
Roles change, places change, still waiting for you

If I had to say what you are: every talk, every thought, worth savoring\
You read my heart as your own, writing the bright biography of my life\
Together we make our times and our luck, counted from the day we met\
I dare shoulder every crisis, because the one behind me is you\
Together we learn the ways of the world, and if one day I no longer fly\
At the age of strolls in the park, the one walking with me is you\
If someday we can still pose for a photo, hair gone thin, it will still be wonderful\
Grown heavy, eyebrows fading, none of the looks matter\
Together we make our times and our luck, counted from the day we met\
Even when I am angry and unreasonable, the one who soothes me is you\

When I meet someone new, I bring it up: the wife at home is you\
