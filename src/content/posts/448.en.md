Please don't see through what weighs on my heart

Let me go on keeping silent

Please don't ask about my past  
Let me go on drowning  
Please don't disturb me in the deep of night  
Let me go on drifting in exile through the dark  
Please don't tell me what love is  
Please let me go on being wrong  
But  
Please believe me  
I do love you  
My dear  
Close your eyes  
Take my hand  
Come with me  
You will never lose your way
