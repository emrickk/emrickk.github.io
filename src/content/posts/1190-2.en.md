At the supermarket behind the office.

Clerk: Excuse me, is this bottle of red wine a gift, or for yourself? Would you like me to wrap it up for you?

Me: It's for drinking at home. Please just wrap it in a sheet of newspaper.

Also, it's a bottle of vinegar.
