description: 'A few stray lines leaning on Gu Cheng''s poem "Far and Near", ending with an I-miss-you said to the sky.'

Been through it, known pain, known joy too

When you look at the clouds, you are very near

When you look at me, you are very far

Then, facing the sky, I say

I miss you so
