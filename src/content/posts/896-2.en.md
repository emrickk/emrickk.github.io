title: 'This Thing Called Money'

Sold off my old portable hard drive and my bicycle. Both at a loss, it seems.

Ah, whatever. For a hundred kuai or so, it isn't worth the fuss.

Remember this: **you can't keep going back and forth on things**

It was clearly a chance to let someone owe you a favor, and I wasted it for nothing. Don't prize it so much, this thing called money.

P.S. Diet, Day1. Dinner: one apple.
