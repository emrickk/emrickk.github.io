title: 'No Such Thing as Crossing the Year'
description: 'There is no such thing as crossing the year; heartless time rolls over us, no questions asked.'
---

As if there were any such thing as crossing into the new year. We never step across the year of our own accord. The year is heartless time rolling over our bodies, no questions asked.
