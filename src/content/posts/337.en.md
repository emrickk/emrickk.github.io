title: 'The Life I Wanted'
---

I originally figured on living as just some ninja or other, earning just enough money. Then marrying a woman who is neither pretty nor ugly, and having two kids, the first a girl, the second a boy.

Once the daughter is married and the son can stand on his own, I would retire from ninja work. After that, an idle, secluded life, playing chess or Go every day. And then dying of old age before my own wife does.

That is just the life I wanted to live.
