title: 'I Only Care About You'
---

If I had never met you

Where would I be now

How would the days be passing

Would life be something to treasure

Perhaps I would know someone

And live an ordinary life

Not knowing whether

There would be love as sweet as honey

Let time hurry past

I only care about you

Glad and willing to be steeped in your breath

How often in one life do you find someone who truly knows you

Losing the strength of life itself would not be a pity

If there came a day

When you said you were about to leave

I would lose myself

Adrift in the boundless sea of people

I ask for no promises

Only every day together

I cannot go on living

On scraps of memory alone

Let time hurry past

Let time hurry past

I only care about you

Glad and willing to be steeped in your breath

How often in one life do you find someone who truly knows you

Losing the strength of life itself would not be a pity

So I beg you

Don't let me leave you

Apart from you I cannot feel

The slightest trace of tenderness
