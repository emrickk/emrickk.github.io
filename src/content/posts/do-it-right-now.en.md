Execution, which can also be understood as the power to push things forward, is one of the most important abilities a product manager can have

When you finish the planning and proposal for a product, there is in fact still a long road between you and the finished product, and what carries you to the finish line quickly and efficiently is exactly this driving force.

Development has no development time, UI claims their schedule is packed solid; none of that is really why a product fails to launch on time. Sorting out the tasks every day and pushing their progress forward is extraordinarily important
