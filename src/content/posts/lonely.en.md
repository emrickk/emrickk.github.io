title: 'Solitude'
description: 'In solitude you can be entirely yourself: solitude is a rare ability, and enviable are those with the inner resources to enjoy it.'
---

When you are alone you can be entirely yourself, because no one else is there to constrain you; you can stretch out freely. Solitude is a particular kind of ability, and people who have it are rare. I have always envied those who possess inner resources, who can enjoy being alone
