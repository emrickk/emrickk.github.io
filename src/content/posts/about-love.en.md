title: 'Ordinary Yet Unforgettable'
description: 'A small thought: express love whenever you can, and life becomes ordinary yet unforgettable.'
---

In short, express love whenever you are able to, give off a little warmth whenever you are able to, and treat both as the most natural things in the world. Live like that, and life becomes something perfectly ordinary yet etched into the bone, I suppose.
