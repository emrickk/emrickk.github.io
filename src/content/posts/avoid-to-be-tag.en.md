title: 'On Labels'
description: 'On trying my hardest to avoid every label, and wondering whether that is a good thing.'
---

I've been trying, as if my life depended on it, to keep any kind of label from being stuck on me: an XX fan, a brain-dead XX stan, XX style, part of the XX scene. I don't know whether that's good or bad. Maybe in the end the only fix the people around me will have on me is: this guy is a dumbass. And nothing else.
