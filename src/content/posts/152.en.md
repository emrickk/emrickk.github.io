title: 'Evil'
---

Ignorance is evil; stupidity is evil; doing harm is evil. There are things in this world where the cause matters more than the result, chasing a dream, for instance. And there are things where the result forever outweighs the cause, hurting another person, for instance.   
The greatest evil in this world often runs rampant in the name of kindness. And the greatest accomplices of the wicked are often those good souls who never need to make any actual sacrifice for the kindness they profess.   
And the most laughable thing in this world is surely this: kindness itself, of all things, unable to move a single step because of the name of kindness.
