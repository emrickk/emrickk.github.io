title: 'Fine, I''ll Just Be Dull'
---

Fine, I'll just be a dull person

Fine, I'll just be a person without moods

Fine, I'll just be a person who quietly works hard

Fine, I'll just be a person who doesn't express himself

Fine, I'll just be a person who might drop a spoiler at any moment

Fine, I'll just be a person whose steps are steady yet light

Fine, I'll just be a person who curbs his desires, forbearing and tough

Fine, I'll just be a person who can't do sentimental and doesn't get romance

Fine, I'll just be a person whose one remark can turn a whole crowd cold

Fine, I'll just be a person who can't do many things yet handles all of it with ease

Fine, I'll just be a person with no honeyed words who forever sticks to the facts

Fine, I'll just be a person who gives no reaction when someone pours out their heart

Fine, I'll just be a person who is perfectly courteous yet never gets close to anyone

Fine, I'll just be the person sipping tea off to one side while the table trades toasts
