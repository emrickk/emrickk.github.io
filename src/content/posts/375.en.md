title: 'More Than a Camera'
description: 'Ansel Adams on photography: what we bring to it is every book we have read, every film we have seen, every person we have loved.'

We don't take pictures with a camera alone. What we bring to photography is every book we have ever read. Every film we have seen. Every piece of music we have heard. Every person we have loved.
