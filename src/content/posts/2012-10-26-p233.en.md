title: 'Goodbye'
---

Watching the films you mentioned, going to the restaurants you had been to, keeping every note you ever wrote me, remembering the look on your face each time you looked at me. I love these utterly meaningless things, drowning in my own wishful affection.

But however lovely the past was, it is still only the past.

Goodbye.
