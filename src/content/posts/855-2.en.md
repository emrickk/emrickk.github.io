On his first visit to China after winning re-election, Obama had a pleasant meeting with the state leaders. The details went more or less like this:

"Hello, I am Zhuxi (Chairman) Xi Jinping."

"Hello, I am Zongli (Premier) Li Keqiang."

"......Fine. I am Zongtong (President) Tongbama."
