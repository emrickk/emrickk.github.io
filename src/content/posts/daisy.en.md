title: 'Daisy'
description: 'A repost of the French poet Alfred de Musset''s poem "Daisy".'
---

I love, and say nothing; I only watch you smiling there across from me;

I love, and it is enough that my heart knows; I need not learn what your heart thinks of me;

I treasure my secret, and I treasure the faint sorrow,

the sorrow that has never turned to pain; I swear: loving you, I give you up,

holding no hope, yet not without happiness; to be able to remember is happiness enough,

even if I can no longer see you, smiling there across from me.
