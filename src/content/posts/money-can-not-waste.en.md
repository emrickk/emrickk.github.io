**Life:** No way to go running, so I've gotten fat again The little submarine I bought hasn't been taken out once since the day it arrived =.= After the last few rains I caught a good number of termites with the water-basin method and did away with every one of them

**Work:** Started building the domestic version of CM Security. I went in full of confidence, never expecting it to turn out to be yet another shameless cash-grab edition, which doesn't make me very happy Of course, dancing in shackles can actually be pretty satisfying in its own way
