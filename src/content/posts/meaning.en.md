title: 'Meaning'
---

By day I lodge in other people's flesh, to fill up my pallid emptiness. By night I drift at the highest point of the city, savoring the stillness of the dark.

Eternal existence: once it was so far out of reach. Yet once possessed, all it brings is emptiness without end.

What drifts past my eyes is nothing but surging sin. This filthy world, these counterfeit human hearts!

Night can bring a numbing stillness, but it cannot cover the stirring of wicked thoughts in the hearts of men.

Haha, I will set their desires alight. Fight, then. Kill. Let all of you die roasting in the fires of greed! Once more I shall have plump, well-fattened souls for my feast.

Come, receive the baptism, become an existence eternal. You will never again know hunger, cold, or pain, or even love and hate.
