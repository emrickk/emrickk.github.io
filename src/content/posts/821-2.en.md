title: 'Encourage the Stone Throwers'
description: 'A repost on the people who crouch in the dark throwing stones at your life, and why ignoring them is the ultimate injury.'
---

You should go and encourage the people who crouch in the dark throwing stones at your life. Whether they scheme against you on their own or smear you on someone's orders, it makes no difference. It may look like you can't strike back and can't argue, but little do they know you grasped the truth of the universe long ago: ignoring an idiot is the ultimate injury you can do to an idiot. If your life had none of them in it, it would surely be a little too perfect, and a shade pale and dull for it.
