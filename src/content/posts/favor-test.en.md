title: 'Picky About What You Love'
description: 'Picky about what you love: someone who truly loves tea will never drink just any tea.'
---

Whatever you love, you are picky about; that is well said. If someone says he loves tea, be sure to ask him which kind. A person who truly loves tea would never drink just any tea.
