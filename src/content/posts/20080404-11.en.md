title: 'Lonely Love'
description: 'A reposted poem about loneliness and love: a park, the rain, and laughter that breaks through the solitude.'
---

In a lonely park, a love is kept\
heedless of clouds in the sky or none\
heedless of dust on the ground, settling or rising\
When the scent of flowers passes by, loneliness too is love

Who says the lonely don't understand love\
Who says the lonely cannot love\
Who says the lonely are only lonely

If a wutong tree were torn up\
the rain might swagger too much\
If your laughter were lost\
love would go too dark\
All the way you came, the notes of love\
Since it is love, let it grow as it will

In the park, I stop where I am\
When the rain comes, I lose my raincoat\
One laugh from you, just one\
and I break through the loneliness of the rain

If I lost my eyes\
if the world lost you\
surely the suffering would be the same

With my hand I touch your beauty\
once, just once\
it is a message of love and longing\
passing from your face over my whole body\
a whole life, it is a whole life
