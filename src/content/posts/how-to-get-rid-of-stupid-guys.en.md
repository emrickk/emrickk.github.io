**Never, ever try to educate those fifty- or sixty-something "elders" you barely know =。=**

When these creatures discuss university with you, they don't actually care whether your school is genuinely impressive or where it ranks in the country, and when they discuss work with you, they don't care whether your industry is a rising one, or how it differs from the traditional ones either

They're just seizing a chance to broadcast their own values, just trying to please themselves, so at moments like this all you need to do is raise the cup in your hand and say loudly, right, right, you're absolutely right
