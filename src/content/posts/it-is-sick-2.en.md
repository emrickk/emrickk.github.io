title: 'Obsessing Over Visible Features Is a Sickness'
description: 'Visible features matter, but they cannot truly change a product: pouring core resources into polishing them again and again is meaningless.'
---

True, visible features do matter, but visible features cannot truly change a product. Pouring your core resources and time into polishing visible features, round after round, means nothing for actually improving the product.
