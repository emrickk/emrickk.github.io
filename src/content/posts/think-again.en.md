title: 'Three Daily Questions'
description: 'Three daily questions for myself: do users really need this, what happens after it ships, and is there data to back it up.'
---

Each day I examine myself on three counts.

"Does the user actually fucking need this thing? Is it a hard need? Is it high frequency?"

again: "Once this thing is built, what can the user do with it? And once they're done, what can I do?"

final: "Fine. Even if you really believe all that, have you produced the data to back this thing up?"
