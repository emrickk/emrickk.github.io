title: 'An Old Lover'
description: 'A quip about running into an old lover at the happiest of moments; I never carry small change.'
---

At the moment of our greatest joy, or in a convenience store, or by the roadside, we will run into your former lover. When that happens, don't look at me; I never carry small change.
