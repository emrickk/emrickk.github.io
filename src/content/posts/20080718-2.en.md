title: 'I Am Already Old, I Am Still Young'
description: 'A few lines blaming exam-driven schooling for a youth never lived, and a vow to keep a young heart.'
---

Always lived by the rules\
never been young\
I don't like this state of being\
this damned exam-driven education\
I will keep my heart young, always\
throw myself wholly, intently, into whatever I am doing\
and pay no mind to anyone else
