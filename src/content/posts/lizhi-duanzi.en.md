title: 'Inspirational One-Liners'
description: 'Inspirational one-liners are easy to write, sound wiser the longer you chew on them, and help your life not at all.'
---

Writing inspirational one-liners is extremely easy.

Take this chestnut: "Very often, what you think is a money problem is actually a people problem. Very often, what you think is a people problem is actually a money problem."

Well? Doesn't it feel truer the longer you chew on it?

But does knowing this truth help your life in any way?

No.
