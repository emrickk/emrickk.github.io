title: 'Scant Knowledge, Baseless Courage'
description: 'The scanter the knowledge, the more absolute the conviction: baseless courage comes from never hearing an opposing view.'
---

The poorer a person's knowledge, the easier it is for them to carry an inexplicable courage and sense of pride. The poorer the knowledge, the more absolute the beliefs - because they have simply never heard or seen any view that stands against their own
