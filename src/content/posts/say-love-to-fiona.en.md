title: 'The Asking Is the Confession'
description: 'Only someone who loves you keeps asking "do you love me"; take every asking as a confession.'
---

Only someone who loves you will ask again and again, "Do you love me?" So you may as well take every one of those askings as a confession of love: the more they ask, the more they care and the deeper the attachment. Thinking of it this way, how could you ever grow impatient? You would be too busy being glad.
