title: 'So'
description: 'A short poem: the number nine bus platform, mild sunlight, and the seam the Creator left showing.'
---

In sunlight just this mild I walked over

Only the number nine bus stops at this platform

Two yuan for the air-conditioned bus

One fifty for the ordinary one

I hoped the one coming would be an ordinary bus

The passing traffic gave off a metallic sheen

This world seems to be fake

The Creator, it seems, did his best with the calculations

and still left a seam showing
