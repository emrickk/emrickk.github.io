title: 'Now'

Everyone has been through all sorts of different things in the past, yet some of them look impossibly happy. That is because they all understand perfectly well what a losing bargain it is to let the past hold you back from enjoying the present.

So live your days cheerfully, and stop pulling a long face all the time. Let yourself relax and meet everything with a smile. It's like how we fool around all day: it isn't that we've forgotten to grow up, nor that we want to forget to grow up. We just wish for a little more time, like children who play until the sun goes down and still can't bear to go home. But the night does come.
