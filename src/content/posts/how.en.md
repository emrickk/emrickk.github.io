title: 'The Measure'
---

When you launch a new feature, how do you set expectations for its numbers? Before conversion and retention reach that impossible "100%", how do you judge whether the product's current numbers are actually good? And once your core metrics have overtaken the competitors or the industry average, how do you decide, from the data or from the product itself, whether you should keep optimizing?
