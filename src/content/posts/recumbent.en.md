title: 'Swallow the Broken Teeth'
description: 'A note to self: most of it is self-inflicted, but swallow the broken teeth with the blood and guard your words and temper.'
---

Most of the time a result like this is self-inflicted. Or rather: it's these acts, which so obviously lead to self-inflicted suffering, that bring about this self-inflicted result.

But remember: when your teeth get knocked out, always swallow them down with the blood.

-----Guard your words, your deeds, and your temper. Remember it, remember it.
