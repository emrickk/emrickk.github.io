title: 'Come Hear Me Curse'
description: 'A repost of a dialogue in which a mother, nearly eighty, explains why she curses people all day.'
---

Feng: "You curse them all day long. How could they feel good about that?"

Mother: "Why don't they ask themselves why I curse them all day?"

Feng: "People past forty can hardly change anymore. What good does your cursing do?"

Mother: "It soothes my heart... I'm past seventy, pushing eighty. How much can I change? And you came all this way to hear me curse people. That's a comfort to me."
