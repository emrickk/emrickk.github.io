description: 'A short product note on click counts: the point is not capping the number of clicks but making each click thoughtless.'
---

I think the heart of the matter is not that the number of clicks absolutely must be kept under some limit, but that users should be able to click without using their brains. The point is to carry users toward where they want to go, and to let them know they really are on the right path.
