When I was young, I always felt full of confidence one moment and stripped of every bit of it the next. I would imagine myself utterly incapable, devoid of charm, without worth. And at the same time I felt I was born to my talents, and that success was only a matter of counting down the days. When I was brimming with confidence, I could overcome even the greatest difficulties; yet even the most trifling slip would convince me that I was still good for nothing. (Bernhard Schlink)
