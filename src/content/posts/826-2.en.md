title: 'One More Stretch of the Road'
---

My friend, hearts can be treacherous and this world is a hard place to live in. I cannot reach the final stop of life together with you, but while our roads still run together I will do everything in my power to help you, and when the time comes for us to part, I will put it off for one more day wherever a day can be gained, and keep you company for one more stretch wherever a stretch can be walked.
