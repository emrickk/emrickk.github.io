description: 'An excerpt from Shi Tiesheng''s "Notes on Principles", on mountains, waters, and the longing to love.'
---

I once walked past mountains, walked past waters, when in truth I was only passing through my own life by way of them; I looked at the sky, looked at the earth, when in truth I was only fixing my own position by way of them; I loved her, and loved you, when in truth I was only fulfilling my longing to love by way of other people. (Shi Tiesheng, Notes on Principles)
