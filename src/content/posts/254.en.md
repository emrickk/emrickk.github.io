title: 'The Days to Come'
description: 'The closing passage of Romain Rolland''s "Jean-Christophe": Saint Christopher crossing the river with the days to come on his shoulder.'
---

Saint Christopher crossed the river, and he asked the child on his shoulder: "Child, who are you, really? Why are you so heavy?" The child answered: "I am the days to come."
