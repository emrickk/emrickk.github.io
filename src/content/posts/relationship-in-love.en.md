title: 'Love in Earnest'
---

I have come to see, deeply, that no one is perfect. In love, the time two people spend in each other's company is stretched out without limit, and flaws get magnified without limit. But the "person" themselves matters more than anything else. So in a relationship, whether the other becomes more beautiful because of you depends on whether you hold that beautiful hope, that faith in being willing to give. As long as you love them sincerely and give your love, then whatever the outcome, the process is bound to be beautiful.
